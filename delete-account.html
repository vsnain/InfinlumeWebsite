Your account and data will be permanently deleted

What gets deleted:

  • Your account profile and login credentials
  • All learning progress and history
  • Quiz scores and achievements
  • Personalized learning preferences
  • Any other data associated with your account

Important Information

  • Processing Time: Deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days.
  • Irreversible: Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered. You'll need to create a new account if you wish to use InfinLume again.
  • Confirmation: You'll receive an email confirmation once your account has been deleted.
